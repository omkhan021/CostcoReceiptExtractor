# Privacy Policy — Costco Receipt Finder

**Effective date:** May 17, 2026

KT Solutions Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") built the **Costco Receipt Finder** app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Costco Account Credentials
To sync your purchase history, you provide your Costco.com email address and password. These credentials are stored **only on your device** using the operating system's encrypted secure storage (Android Keystore). They are never transmitted to our servers — they are used solely to authenticate directly with Costco's website on your behalf.

### Receipt and Purchase Data
Receipt and item data retrieved from your Costco account is stored **locally on your device** in an encrypted database. This data does not leave your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

### Search History and Usage Data
Search queries you enter are processed entirely on-device. We do not collect or transmit your search terms. A local count of daily searches is stored on-device to enforce the free tier limit.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob (Free Users)
If you use the free tier of the app, banner advertisements are displayed by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. We request non-personalized ads only. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Google Play Billing
Premium upgrades are processed through **Google Play In-App Billing**. We do not receive or store your payment information. All payment processing is handled by Google. See Google Play's Terms of Service for details.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above solely to:

- Authenticate with Costco.com to retrieve your purchase receipts
- Store and display your receipt data locally on your device
- Enforce free-tier search limits locally on your device
- Display advertisements to free-tier users via Google AdMob

We do **not** sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to any third party.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

All personal data (credentials, receipts, search counts) is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate servers that store your data. As a result:

- We cannot access your Costco credentials or receipt data
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device
- You can clear app data at any time via your device's Settings

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
